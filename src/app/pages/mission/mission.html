NHCRF Foundation

Mission & Vision

Protecting and promoting human rights across Bangladesh through transparency, accountability, and justice.

Our Mission

The National Human Rights Crime Reporters Foundation (NHCRF) is committed to documenting, reporting, and advocating against human rights violations across Bangladesh. We empower citizens by providing legal guidance, raising public awareness, and ensuring that every voice is heard regardless of social status.

Our Vision

A Bangladesh where every citizen enjoys their fundamental rights, where rule of law is supreme, and where justice is accessible to all. We envision a society built on equality, dignity, and the protection of all human rights as enshrined in the UN Universal Declaration of Human Rights.
